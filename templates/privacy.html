Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account with Spendly, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and an encrypted password. As you use the Service, we also store the expense data you choose to record — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect any payment, banking, or financial account information; Spendly is a manual tracking tool only.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Service: to authenticate your account, display your expenses back to you, generate summaries and category breakdowns, and improve the product experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers. We may use anonymized, aggregate usage statistics to understand how Spendly is used and to guide product decisions.

3. Data Storage

Your account information and expense records are stored on secure servers with industry-standard safeguards in place. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You may request export or deletion of your data at any time from your account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third-party services for essential infrastructure, such as hosting and email delivery. These providers only receive the data necessary to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy commitments. We do not embed advertising trackers or analytics that share your personal expense data with external parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to access or delete your data, or wish to raise a concern about how your information is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
